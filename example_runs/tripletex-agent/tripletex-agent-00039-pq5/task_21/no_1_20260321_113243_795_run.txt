### Key Design Decisions:
- **Employment details are embedded inline** within the `POST /employee/employment` call via the `employmentDetails` array, saving a separate `POST /employee/employment/details` call.

- **Standard time** must be a separate call as it cannot be embedded in the employee or employment creation.
- **No verification GETs** are needed — 201 responses confirm success.
